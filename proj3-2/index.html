Part 3. Environment Lightl

Pick one *.exr* file to use for all subparts here. Include a converted *.jpg* of it in your website so we know what map you are using.

In a few sentences, explain the ideas behind environment lighting (i.e. why we do it/how it works).

The idea behind environment lighting is to light the objects from a background. This is done by taking a texture and supplying it as incident radiance from all directions on the sphere. Because the enviroment lighting is treated as being infinitely far away, it appears as the background of the image. This allows us to paste images of a field or a building in the background of the image, and have the object's lighting reflect that without having to build the field or building as objects in the scene. Becuase they are treated instead as light sources, it is much simpler to get cool results.
